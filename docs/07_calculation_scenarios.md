# 07. Calculation Scenarios

The platform supports several high-level scenarios that reflect real-world investor behavior.

## 1. Single Purchase (Buy & Hold)
- **Input:** 10,000 PLN into EDO on Jan 2024.
- **Goal:** See the value at maturity (10 years later).
- **Output:** Total net profit, effective annual interest rate, and inflation-adjusted value.

## 2. Early Withdrawal (The "Emergency" Fund)
- **Input:** 10,000 PLN into COI. Withdraw after 18 months.
- **Goal:** Understand the cost of liquidity.
- **Output:** Net amount received after the 0.70 PLN/bond fee and 19% tax. Comparison vs. keeping the money in a 0% savings account.

## 3. The Bond Ladder (Monthly Savings)
- **Input:** 1,000 PLN invested every month for 5 years into different bonds.
- **Goal:** Create a consistent stream of maturing bonds or payouts.
- **Output:** Total portfolio growth over time and a "Liquidity Schedule" showing when money becomes available.

## 4. The Inflation Stress-Test
- **Input:** 5,000 PLN into EDO.
- **Goal:** What happens if inflation is 5% vs. 15% vs. 2%?
- **Output:** A multi-line chart showing "Net Value" under different CPI scenarios.

## 5. The Rollover (Re-investment)
- **Input:** A maturing 3-year TOS bond.
- **Goal:** Roll it into a new EDO bond using the "Zamiana" discount.
- **Output:** The 0.10 PLN discount per bond applied to the new purchase and its impact on the long-term yield.

## 6. Comparison: Bonds vs. Alternatives
- **Input:** 10,000 PLN.
- **Scenario:** Bond (EDO) vs. S&P 500 (Historical Average).
- **Goal:** Visualize the "Volatility Premium."
- **Output:** Two lines on a chart. The Bond line is smooth; the S&P 500 line is jagged. Show "Max Drawdown" for the equity path.
