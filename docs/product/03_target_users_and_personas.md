# 03. Target Users & Personas

Understanding our users is critical to designing a UI that balances precision with accessibility.

## Primary User Personas

### 1. "The Safety Seeker" (Agnieszka, 34)
- **Background:** Professional with stable income, holds significant savings in a standard bank account.
- **Motivation:** Tired of seeing her savings lose value to inflation but fears the volatility of the stock market.
- **Goal:** Wants to understand how EDO (10-year) bonds work and if they are "truly safe."
- **Key Pain Point:** Confused by the calculation of interest in the 2nd year and beyond. Needs to see a clear timeline of payouts.

### 2. "The Strategic Planner" (Marek, 42)
- **Background:** Financially literate, already invests in some stocks, wants to diversify into bonds for a portion of his retirement portfolio.
- **Motivation:** Wants to optimize his "bond ladder" to ensure he has liquidity every year while maximizing returns.
- **Goal:** Compare EDO bonds vs. COI vs. TOS over a 12-year horizon.
- **Key Pain Point:** Hard to calculate the "rollover discount" and compounding effect manually. Needs advanced simulation tools.

### 3. "The Goal-Oriented Saver" (Kuba, 28)
- **Background:** Just starting his career, saving for a first apartment down-payment in 5 years.
- **Motivation:** Needs a predictable outcome.
- **Goal:** Wants to know exactly how much he will have in 60 months if he invests 2,000 PLN monthly.
- **Key Pain Point:** Doesn't know the difference between "Capitalization" and "Payout." Needs simple, visual summaries of "Final Amount."

## User Expectations & Fears

| Expectation | How we meet it |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Trustworthy Data** | Direct integration with official GUS/NBP rates. |
| **Simplicity** | Minimalist UI with progressive disclosure of complexity. |
| **Mobile Access** | Fully responsive calculators for on-the-go checking. |
| **No Hidden Fees** | Transparent display of early redemption penalties. |

| Fear | How we mitigate it |
| :--- | :--- |
| **"I'll lose money"** | Clearly show the "Capital Protection" nature of bonds. |
| **"It's too complex"** | Use plain-language summaries: "You get X after Y years." |
| **"Data Breach"** | No mandatory account; local simulation by default. |

## Target Segments
1.  **Retail Investors:** People looking for alternatives to bank deposits.
2.  **Parents:** Planning for children's future (Family bonds - ROS/ROD).
3.  **Retirees:** Looking for stable income streams from coupon payouts.
4.  **Financial Educators:** Using the tool to explain principles of inflation and interest.
